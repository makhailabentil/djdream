Party Prep Guide

How To Set Up A Smooth, Stress-Free Celebration

Space Setup

Keep one clear activity area where children can gather, move, and see the performer comfortably.

  • Open floor area for games and dancing
  • Chairs moved back for safe movement

Timing Window

Schedule character entertainment before cake so energy and attention stay high during activities.

  • Character set starts early in the event
  • Cake and gifts follow after guided activities

Guest Readiness

Let parents know the party includes interactive segments so kids arrive ready to participate.

  • Comfortable clothes for active moments
  • Any sensory preferences shared in advance

Photo Plan

Choose one dedicated photo moment so every child gets a picture without interrupting the flow.

  • Designate a quick lineup order
  • Keep one adult as photo coordinator
